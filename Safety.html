FABLab Safety

Attire

  1. Long hair must be tied up
  2. No loose items allowed
  3. No long sleeves
  4. Hands free of adornments
  5. Long pants
  6. Covered shoes

Housekeeping practices

  1. After using the tools, always return it back to their designated positions in the tool racks.
  2. Always keep the workplace neat and tidy after use.
  3. Remove debris and chips off the machines or workbenches after use.
  4. Recycle unused or leftover materials at the recycling area.

Emergency Procedures

In the event of an emergency, seek assistance from the staff in-charge, if staff is not around, call,
  1. SP Emergency Hotline 6772-1234
  2. Ambulance and SCDF 995
  3. Police 999
